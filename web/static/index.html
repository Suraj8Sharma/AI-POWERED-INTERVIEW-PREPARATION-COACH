How it works

Three steps to a full mock session.

1

Choose a role

Select your target role from the sidebar. PrepLoom tailors the question mix — behavioural and technical — to what that role actually tests.

2

Answer via voice or text

Speak naturally or type your answer. Whisper transcribes speech, your webcam tracks presence, and the question is read aloud if you want.

3

Get your report

After the session you get per-question scores across technical, communication, and confidence dimensions — with actionable improvement tips.
