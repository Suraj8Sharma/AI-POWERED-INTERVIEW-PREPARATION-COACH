Role-aware questions

Questions built around your target role.

Pull from a structured RAG-powered bank aligned to your target role. Each session mixes behavioural and technical prompts.

01 Question bank

Structured, role-specific question sets.

PrepLoom pulls from a curated ChromaDB vector store, mixing 6–7 technical questions with 3 behavioural ones every session. No two sessions are identical.

  • Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) question fetch
  • Technical + behavioural balance every session
  • Difficulty and subtopic shown per question
  • Ideal answers available on demand

Answer evaluation

LLM scoring that actually explains itself.

Submit spoken or typed answers for feedback on reasoning and accuracy. Ideal answers reveal a strong baseline to compare against.

02 Evaluation

Strengths, gaps, and what you missed.

Every answer gets a technical score, a list of strengths, areas to improve, and missing points — not just a number. The feedback is actionable on the same day.

  • 0–100 technical score per answer
  • Streengths and improvement points called out
  • Missing key concepts surfaced explicitly
  • Short qualitative feedback paragraph

Communication signals

Notice how you sound before the real room does.

Filler words, pacing, and structure — the signals a hiring manager notices without naming them.

03 Communication

NLP analysis on every answer.

PrepLoom counts fillers, calculates your words-per-minute, and scores clarity and structure — not just vocabulary. The goal is to make habits visible before they cost you.

  • Filler word detection (um, uh, like, you know)
  • Speaking pace in WPM with ideal range
  • Clarity and structure score
  • Whisper-powered transcription
🗣️
Communication score

Video & posture

Presence is a skill. Practice it.

Optional camera-based signals on openness, engagement, and posture — a nudge, not a verdict. Comfort still comes first.

04 Presence

Live body language via MediaPipe.

A WebSocket streams your webcam frames to a pose-estimation backend. You get real-time openness, engagement, posture, and fidgeting signals — updated every frame while you speak.

  • MediaPipe pose estimation
  • 4 live signals: openness, engagement, posture, fidgeting
  • WebSocket continuous analysis (~15 FPS)
  • Confidence score feeds into the final report
