Features

PrepLoom is built around how real interviews are scored: substance, signal, and presence. Here is what you get in the practice room today.

Role-aware technical questions

Draw from a structured bank aligned to your target role. Each session mixes behavioural and technical prompts so you rehearse both storytelling and depth.

Answer evaluation

Submit spoken or typed answers for feedback on reasoning and accuracy. Ideal answers can be revealed when you want to compare against a strong baseline.

Communication signals

Metrics such as clarity and structure help you notice habits—rambling, filler, or pacing—before a hiring manager does.

Video and posture (optional)

Enable your camera for lightweight cues on openness, engagement, and posture. Use it as a nudge, not a verdict—comfort still comes first.
