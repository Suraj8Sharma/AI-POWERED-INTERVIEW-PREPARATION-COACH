Our Mission

Give candidates a single place to rehearse under pressure.

🎯

Focused Practice

Feedback you can act on the same day—whether you are early in a search or days from a final round.

  • Real-time awareness: Connect your camera for posture and presence feedback.
  • AI-driven insights: Let the LLM analyze the depth and structure of your answers.
  • Calm repetitions: Build muscle memory without the high-stakes anxiety.

What's Next

🚀

Coming Soon

While the practice room works without a sign-in today, we are actively building out persistent features.

  • User Accounts: Securely save your session history and past feedback.
  • Revision Corner: Curated technical snippets and cheat sheets to review before testing.
  • Team Mode: Collaborative peer-to-peer mock interview scheduling.
