About PrepLoom

We believe preparation should feel focused and repeatable—not frantic. PrepLoom is a capstone-grade workspace for mock interviews: technical depth, how you sound, and how you show up.

Mission

Give candidates a single place to rehearse under pressure, with feedback they can act on the same day—whether they are early in a search or days from a final round.

What’s next

User accounts, saved sessions, and team features will land once you decide on data storage and authentication. The practice room is ready to use without signing in.

Contact

For academic or project questions, use the channels your team already uses. Product feedback from real practice sessions is especially welcome.
